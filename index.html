Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the Arnold Gym Management System application.

The Arnold Gym Management System app is designed to help gym administrators manage members, track memberships, and monitor gym activity.

Information We Collect

The application may collect and store the following information for gym management purposes:

  • Member name
  • Phone number
  • Address
  • Membership plan
  • Membership fees and payment details
  • Membership start and expiry dates

This information is used only for managing gym memberships within the application.

How We Use Information

The collected information is used to:

  • Manage gym members
  • Track membership plans and payments
  • Maintain gym attendance and records
  • Provide administrative management features

Data Storage

The app may use secure cloud services such as Firebase to store and manage application data. These services follow industry-standard security practices.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share user information with third parties. Data is only used for the functionality of the gym management system.

Security

We value your trust in providing information and strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it.

Children's Privacy

This application is not intended for use by children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Users are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
